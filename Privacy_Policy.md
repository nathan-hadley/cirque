# Privacy Policy

This app uses your location to display your position on the map. Location is
not collected, stored, or shared.

When you submit a new problem through the Contribute screen, you provide a
contact name and email address. These are stored with the submission so we can
review it and follow up if needed. They are visible only to the app's
administrators, are never shown in the app or shared with third parties, and
are included in routine backups that are retained for 30 days. To have your
submission contact details removed, email nathan.hadley@me.com.

No other data is collected, stored, or shared.
